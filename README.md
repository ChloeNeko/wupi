## ✨ Welcome to your new OS!
<br>

Meet **Wupi**! your new digital cat maid and best friend. She lives right inside your chat and handles all the boring stuff in the background. She makes sure characters don't get amnesia, keeps track of your world's rules, and manages your inventory. You just sit back, relax, and focus on your story!

<br>
<br>
<br>

## 🎀 The 5 Engines of **WUPCM**
### Everything is perfectly organized into a hub menu!
<br>

🌍 **World**: Tracks the weather, locations, and the rules of your universe!
<br>

💖 **User**: All about YOU and the stuff in your inventory.
<br>

🚂 **Perform**: The core of the system and your friendly cat-maid assistant, **WUPI**!
<br>

🎲 **Chaos**: Adds fun random events to keep the story spicy!
<br>

💾 **Memory**: The actual brain that tracks your quests, relationships, and history.

## 🌟 Super Cool Features
<br>

- [x] **No More Yapping!** The system keeps things strict so the AI stops rambling and making things up! <br><br>
- [x] **Totally Modular!** Don't need the Chaos engine today? Just turn it off with one click! No annoying restarts needed! <br><br>
- [x] **Background Magic!** Wupi updates all your lore and memories silently in the background so it never ruins your actual roleplay chat! <br><br>
- [x] **Perfect Consistency!** Your characters will finally remember exactly who you are, what you look like, and what you did together 100 messages ago! <br><br>
- [x] **You Don't Need to Be a Coder!** Everything is built right into a cute little menu!
